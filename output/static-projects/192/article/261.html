Ingredients

Gather these fresh ingredients for a delicious meal.

Using high-quality chicken and fresh vegetables will enhance the flavor of the dish. Opt for whole grain couscous for added nutrients, and fresh herbs to elevate the overall taste.

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and garlic, sauté until translucent.
  3. Add diced chicken, season with cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Cook until browned.
  4. Stir in mixed vegetables and cook for another 3-4 minutes.
  5. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  6. Stir in couscous, cover, and remove from heat. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  7. Fluff with a fork and garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

Tips & Tricks

To achieve a fluffy couscous, make sure to let it steam covered after adding the broth. This allows it to absorb moisture without becoming mushy.

Avoid overcooking the chicken as it can become dry. Also, ensure you use the right amount of broth to couscous ratio for perfect texture.

Serving Suggestions

Serve hot, garnished with parsley and your choice of sides.

FAQs

Q1

Can I use quinoa instead of couscous? Yes, quinoa is a great gluten-free alternative.

Q2

How do I store leftovers? Keep in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.

Q3

Can this be made ahead of time? Yes, prepare everything in advance and just heat before serving.

Q4

What can I substitute for chicken? Tofu or chickpeas can be used for a vegetarian option.
